## Competitive Implications for CollegeDelhi.com

**Where they're strong vs. us**: National scale, 100+ exam predictor breadth, 12 years of accumulated backlinks/domain authority almost certainly dwarfs a Delhi-only site.

**Where we're strong vs. them**: Geographic specificity — collegedelhi.com's entire content strategy (40+ Delhi-NCR-specific MBA/engineering pages with exact fee/placement/cutoff data per college) is something a national generalist platform structurally under-invests in. "Best MBA colleges in Delhi NCR" intent is more precisely served by a Delhi-native site than a 100-exam-wide national predictor tool.

**Opportunities**: Collegedunia's predictor tools are exam-input-driven utilities, not narrative/decision-support content. Our FAQ-rich, comparison-heavy pages (FMS vs IIFT, college-specific admission steps) serve the "which Delhi college is right for me" query intent that a predictor tool answers numerically but not contextually — a real content-format gap to exploit in Delhi-specific long-tail queries.

**Threats**: If Collegedunia decides to deepen Delhi-specific content (they have the financial resources to do it: ₹201 Cr revenue, no funding constraints), their domain authority advantage could let them outrank a smaller site quickly on the same long-tail terms.
